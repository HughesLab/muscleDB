MuscleDB

Most studies to-date have not addressed transcriptional diversity among skeletal muscles. MuscleDB is a project that uses unbiased RNA sequencing (RNA-seq) to profile global mRNA expression in a wide array of smooth, cardiac, and skeletal muscle tissues from mice and rats.
